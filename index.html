About A Dot Tattoo

A Dot Tattoo Studio is led by Arvind Kumar, a professional tattoo artist with 5+ years of experience in creating clean and custom tattoo designs. The studio offers a full range of services including tattoo removal, ear and body piercing, custom tattoos, and expert cover-up work.

Every session is planned with a strong focus on hygiene, precision, and long-lasting clarity. Arvind works closely with clients to understand their ideas and create designs that match their personality and vision. Whether it’s a new tattoo, a correction, or a piercing, the studio ensures a safe and professional experience from start to finish.
